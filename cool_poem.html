My Cool Poem

Cricket Unites a Nation

The men with bats are coming home back with good news.
They are here to distribute bundles of joy on rickshaws to everyone in our neighborhood.
Thank god, our family will stop bickering and the tension will be defused.
Finally, I can be just a kid hanging out with my friends and have a normal childhood.
Sadly, the sweet taste of success eventually leaves everyone’s mouth.
Once again, I get thrown from the clear sunny skies back into the black pit of loneliness.
Suddenly, the fights resume and the commodities of tolerance and serenity go into a drought.
Why can’t we be happy all the time? The attitudes of some are just odious.
Hopefully, the men in bats will come back and hurry soon.
And once again arrive with the magical good news.
So that once again, to all the pain and sorrow, my family can be immune.
Whatever you do, great men with bats, do me a favor and don’t lose.
